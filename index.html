EXPERTISE

Dynamic shots

Tired of static perspectives shot on a tripod in the corner of the room? Not only do we change perspective for every shot but also can we offer smooth camera movements that emphasize the action. Thanks to our experience we can find the most interesting looking angles and don't miss a moment.

Available light

From the beginning, we've been working in dim and harsh lighting conditions. If you're concerned about capturing the atmosphere inside your nightclub or during the moonlight scene, then we can make the best out of it - without disturbing the moment with any artificial light. We favour realism over manually perfecting every little detail.

Editing and Effects

Whether it is an action packed commercial or a frugal documentary, our experiences in 2D and 3D visual effects can enrich your production. When it comes to giving your project the final cut, we can organize a soundtrack for you and enhance the footage with a final colour grade.

Flexibility

We do what it takes to get the shot. Don't hesitate to bring in your crazy ideas, we've probably done similarly unordinary things before. Should it be needed, we always have the possibility of using our contacts to fulfill your special demands.
